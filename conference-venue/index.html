Upmarket and reasonable!

Nestled in amongst beautiful trees, this 3600m² property is conveniently situated off Westville Road, in the heart of central Westville, Durban, and provides a private and relaxed environment for delegates. There is off-street parking for over 20 cars.

  • 2 x data projector screens (you can orientate the training either end of the room)
  • 2 x flip charts
  • 1 x wall chart
  • A data projector is available on request
  • FREE WiFi for delegates attending training
  • Off-street parking for approximately 20 cars
  • Back-up generator for load shedding
  • Liquor licence
  • Excellent catering
