Welcome to 18 Burlington

Upmarket and reasonable! Nestled in amongst beautiful trees, this 3600m2 property is conveniently situated off Westville Road, in the heart of central Westville, Durban. Six B&B rooms, in a gracious double-storey house, provide a private and relaxed environment for guests.
There is off-street parking for over 20 cars, and a beautiful pool and patio area leading off from the large dining room.
There are 6 en-suite guest rooms with every convenience. The house is exclusive use for our guests.

Our Facilities


Parking

18 Burlington offers ample secure, off-street parking for all our guests.

WiFi

WiFi Internet Access is available in all rooms throughout. Complimentary access is provided on a daily basis.

Television

Flatscreen televisions are available in all rooms with access to the DSTV Hotel Bouquet and CNN.


Swimming

We have a lovely pool available for guests’ use.   .

Walking

We are within walking distance to Westville CBD's shops, restaurants, etc.

Conference Facilities

Our conference room has air conditioning, dimmable lighting and comes standard with all necessary equipment.



Attractions

We are a mere 20 minutes or less from the beaches, Durban city centre, International Convention Centre Durban (ICC), Durban Exhibition Centre, Suncoast Casino, Durban Golf Courses, Umhlanga, and 10 minutes or less to the CBD of Pinetown, Westville Hospital, and the Westway & Westend Office Parks next to the Pavilion Shopping Centre.
Area Information

20 minutes or less to Durban beaches, Moses Mabhida stadium, ABSA stadium and 30 minutes from the King Shaka International Airport. 10 minutes or less to CBD’s of Pinetown and Durban, the ICC and Durban Exhibition Centre. 5 minutes or less to Westville Pavilion Shopping Centre, Westwood Mall, Westway Office Park, Westend Office Park, Essex Terrace and Westville Hospital. 18 Burlington is well located for easy access from all major freeways and the King Shaka International Airport.
